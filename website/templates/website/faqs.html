General Questions

What is Socialz Water?

+

Socialz Water transforms everyday water bottles into interactive brand experiences through QR codes, connecting brands with consumers while offering instant rewards.

How does it work?

+

Simple: QR integration → Consumer scans → Instant rewards + brand analytics. Every scan creates value for both consumers and brands.

Where are bottles available?

+

Strategic placement across retail shops, colleges, corporate offices, gyms, hotels, restaurants, and events throughout India.

What makes Socialz Water different from traditional advertising?

+

Unlike traditional advertising, we offer direct consumer interaction, instant rewards, real-time measurable results, and turn every bottle into a personalized brand touchpoint.

Is there any minimum order requirement?

+

We accommodate various scales - from small event orders (100+ bottles) to large corporate campaigns (10,000+ bottles). Contact us for a customized quote.
