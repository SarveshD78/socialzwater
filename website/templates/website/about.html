Who We Are

Socialz Water is a pioneering technology company that bridges the gap between sustainable consumption and digital advertising. We've created an innovative platform that transforms everyday water bottles into interactive advertising spaces, allowing consumers to earn rewards while brands connect with their target audience in meaningful ways.

Founded on the principles of sustainability, innovation, and community engagement, we believe that every small action can contribute to a larger positive impact. Our QR-code technology makes advertising more engaging, rewards more accessible, and environmental consciousness more rewarding.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about Socialz Water.

Simply scan the QR code on any Socialz Water bottle with your smartphone camera, watch a short 20-30 second advertisement, and receive instant rewards up to ₹10. It's that easy!

We partner with a diverse range of brands including tech companies, food & beverage brands, lifestyle products, and sustainable businesses that align with our environmental values.

Rewards are processed instantly after watching the advertisement. You can receive payments through UPI, bank transfer, or choose to donate to environmental causes.

Each QR code can be scanned once per unique user. However, you can scan multiple different bottles throughout the day to maximize your earnings!

By encouraging people to engage with water bottles before disposal, we increase awareness about recycling and sustainability. Plus, we partner with brands committed to environmental responsibility.
