About Socialz Water

Revolutionizing water bottle advertising with innovative QR technology

About Us

Who We Are & What We Do

Socialz Water is a pioneering advertising-technology company that transforms everyday water bottles into interactive brand experiences. Through custom labels and QR-enabled campaigns, we help businesses showcase their message in a way that is direct, engaging, and trackable.

At our core, we believe in creating a win-win ecosystem for all stakeholders. Founded on the principles of innovation, accessibility, and engagement, Socialz Water is redefining how India experiences advertising.

Brands benefit from measurable visibility and ROI
Consumers enjoy meaningful rewards with every scan
Partners unlock scalable business opportunities

Mission

To change the way India advertises — one water bottle at a time. With QR-powered labels and real-time rewards, we connect brands and consumers in a way that's engaging, measurable, and unforgettable.

Vision

To make Socialz Water the #1 Pan-India advertising platform, where every sip turns into a brand connection and every campaign delivers real results.
